How we work

At our company, we operate on a project-based model where tasks are assigned to teams based on their expertise and availability. 

When a new project comes in, our project managers assess the requirements and allocate the necessary resources, including assigning specific teams to handle different aspects of the project.

Once the teams are assigned, our project managers work closely with team leaders to define clear objectives and deadlines for each task within the project. These objectives serve as milestones that need to be achieved to ensure the successful completion of the project.

To incentivize performance and ensure accountability, we tie task completion and project milestones to our salary reward system. Each team member has individual objectives related to their assigned tasks, and their performance in meeting these objectives directly impacts their salary rewards.Throughout the project lifecycle, team leaders provide regular feedback and support to team members to help them achieve their objectives efficiently. 

Additionally, our project management software allows for transparent tracking of progress, enabling both team members and management to monitor performance and address any issues promptly.By aligning individual objectives with project goals and tying rewards to performance, we foster a culture of accountability, collaboration, and continuous improvement within our teams. This approach not only ensures the successful delivery of projects but also motivates our employees to strive for excellence in their work.
